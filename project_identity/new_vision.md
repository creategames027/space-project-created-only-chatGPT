# 🌌 New Vision

## Level 4: Concept Transformation

Space Project changes its main purpose.

## Previous Identity

A collection of cosmic systems, notes and experiments.

## New Identity

An AI-powered cosmic research environment where knowledge, simulations and discoveries are connected into one living structure.

## New Principles

- Build tools, not only documents.
- Create interactions, not only databases.
- Connect science and creativity.
- Turn ideas into experiments.

## Direction

The project should feel like a digital space laboratory created for exploring the Universe.
