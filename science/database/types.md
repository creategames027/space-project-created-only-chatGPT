# 🌌 Object Types Guide

A small guide for the astronomy database.

## ⭐ Star
Objects that produce energy through nuclear fusion.

Examples:
- Sun
- Betelgeuse

## 🪐 Planet
Objects that orbit stars.

Examples:
- Earth
- Mars

## 🌌 Galaxy
Huge systems containing stars, gas, dust and dark matter.

Examples:
- Milky Way
- Andromeda

## 🕳️ Black Hole
Regions of space where gravity is extremely strong.

Examples:
- TON 618

## 📡 Phenomenon
Events or signals studied by astronomers.

Examples:
- Fast Radio Bursts
