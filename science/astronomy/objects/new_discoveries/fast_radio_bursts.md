# 📡 Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs)

## Discovery category
Newer and still actively researched astronomical phenomena.

## What are they?

Fast Radio Bursts are extremely short pulses of radio waves coming from distant space.

## Why are they interesting?

- Their exact causes are not fully understood for all FRBs.
- Some are connected with highly magnetic neutron stars called magnetars.
- They can help study the material between galaxies.

## Open questions

- What produces every type of FRB?
- Why do some sources repeat while others do not?
- What can FRBs reveal about the Universe?

## Research status

Scientists continue observing and cataloging new FRB sources.
