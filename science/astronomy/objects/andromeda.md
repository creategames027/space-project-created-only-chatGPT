# 🌌 Andromeda Galaxy (M31)

## Basic information

- Type: Spiral galaxy
- Distance: about 2.5 million light-years
- Location: Andromeda constellation
- Member of: Local Group

## Interesting facts

- It is the nearest large galaxy to the Milky Way.
- It is approaching the Milky Way and will interact with it in the distant future.
- It contains hundreds of billions of stars.

## Observation notes

Useful target for amateur astronomy and astrophotography.
