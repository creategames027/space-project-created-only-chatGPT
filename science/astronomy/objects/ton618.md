# 🕳️ TON 618

## Basic information

- Type: Supermassive black hole candidate
- Distance: billions of light-years
- Location: In the direction of the Canes Venatici constellation
- Estimated mass: around tens of billions of Solar masses

## Interesting facts

- One of the most massive known black hole candidates.
- It is located in the center of a very distant quasar.
- Studying objects like TON 618 helps scientists understand galaxy evolution.

## Research notes

Important object for studying extreme gravity and the early Universe.
