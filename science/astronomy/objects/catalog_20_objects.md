# 🌌 Astronomy Object Catalog #001

First collection of 20 real astronomical objects.

## 1. Sun ☀️
- Type: G-type main-sequence star
- Distance: 1 AU from Earth
- Importance: Source of energy for Earth

## 2. Moon 🌙
- Type: Natural satellite
- Distance: ~384,400 km
- Importance: Earth's closest cosmic neighbor

## 3. Mercury ☿️
- Type: Rocky planet
- Feature: Closest planet to the Sun

## 4. Venus ♀️
- Type: Rocky planet
- Feature: Thick carbon dioxide atmosphere

## 5. Earth 🌍
- Type: Rocky planet
- Feature: Known world with life

## 6. Mars 🔴
- Type: Rocky planet
- Feature: Home of Olympus Mons

## 7. Jupiter 🪐
- Type: Gas giant
- Feature: Largest planet in the Solar System

## 8. Saturn 🪐
- Type: Gas giant
- Feature: Famous ring system

## 9. Uranus
- Type: Ice giant
- Feature: Rotates on its side

## 10. Neptune
- Type: Ice giant
- Feature: Very strong winds

## 11. Pluto
- Type: Dwarf planet
- Feature: Kuiper Belt object

## 13. Orion Nebula (M42)
- Type: Emission nebula

## 14. Andromeda Galaxy (M31)

## 16. Whirlpool Galaxy (M51)

- Feature: Created by a stellar explosion

## 19. Betelgeuse

- Feature: One of the largest visible stars
